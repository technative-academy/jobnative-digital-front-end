About 15Below

15Below is a Brighton-based travel tech company specialising in passenger communications for the airline and rail industries. They build smart notification and disruption management tools used by major global carriers, helping millions of passengers stay informed when things go wrong. Their engineering team works across cloud-native architecture with a strong focus on reliability and scale.
